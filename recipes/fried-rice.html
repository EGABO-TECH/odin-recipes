Fried Rice

Description

This fried rice recipe only takes 15 minutes to cook and tastes just like you get at your favorite Chinese restaurant. Leftover rice, plus a couple of eggs, baby carrots, peas, and soy sauce is all you need. Garnish with sliced green onions, if desired.

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup of chopped baby carrots
  • ½ cup of frozen green peas
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced , or to taste (Optional)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups of leftover cooked and chilled white rice
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce or more to taste
  • 2 teaspoons of sesame oil, or to taste

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Assemble ingredients.

  2. Step 2

    3. Place carrots in a small saucepan and cover with water. Bring to a low boil and cook for 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in peas, then immediately drain in a colander.

  3. Step 3

    4. Heat a wok over high heat. Pour in vegetable oil, then stir in carrots, peas, and garlic; cook for about 30 seconds. Add eggs; stir quickly to scramble eggs with vegetables.

  4. Step 4

    5. Stir in cooked rice. Add soy sauce and toss rice to coat. Drizzle with sesame oil and toss again.

  5. Step 5

    6. Serve hot and enjoy
